INTRIGUING ABSTRACT:
==================================================
The silent threat of compromised product quality in distributed agricultural Internet of Things (IoT) networks demands an intelligent, real-time solution. This paper unveils MASMFA, a novel Multi-Agent System (MAS) meticulously integrated within a robust IoT local network, specifically engineered for real-time milk quality monitoring. Our innovative architecture leverages semantic web for rich information representation, scalable cloud services for server infrastructure, and high-speed LTE technology for seamless communication across a four-level IoT structure. We propose a comprehensive system design featuring smart sensors, dedicated communication and analysis agents, and a sophisticated decision-making subsystem that delivers critical quality alerts directly to farm administrators. MASMFA represents a significant advancement in automated quality control, offering a crucial blueprint for intelligent agricultural systems. By demonstrating the power of MAS to enhance decision-making within complex sensor networks, this work promises to revolutionize product quality assurance and operational efficiency in infocommunication environments, paving the way for scalable, intelligent monitoring solutions across diverse agricultural and industrial sectors.
