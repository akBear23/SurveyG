INTRIGUING ABSTRACT:
==================================================
Rapid and accurate remote sensing image change detection (CD) is critical for diverse applications, from environmental monitoring to disaster response. However, current deep learning approaches often struggle to balance computational efficiency with robust bi-temporal feature extraction and interaction, particularly when dealing with complex land cover changes. We introduce EfficientCD, a novel deep learning framework designed to overcome these limitations, offering a superior balance of accuracy and efficiency for remote sensing CD.

At its core, EfficientCD leverages an EfficientNet backbone for rich, multi-level feature extraction. Our key innovation is the **ChangeFPN**, a parameterless, layer-based Feature Pyramid Network module that employs an alternating selection method to significantly enhance information exchange and interaction between bi-temporal feature maps. This novel approach directly addresses the challenge of representing differential features without adding computational overhead. Furthermore, a sophisticated layer-by-layer decoding strategy, integrating Euclidean distance for multi-level feature fusion, ensures precise reconstruction of change masks. Validated on four benchmark datasets (LEVIR-CD, SYSU-CD, CLCD, WHUCD), EfficientCD demonstrates outstanding performance, achieving superior change detection accuracy while maintaining high computational efficiency. This work presents a significant advancement in remote sensing CD, providing a robust and scalable solution for critical Earth observation tasks.
