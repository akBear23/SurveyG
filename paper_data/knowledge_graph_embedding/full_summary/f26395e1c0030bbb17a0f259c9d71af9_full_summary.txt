INTRIGUING ABSTRACT:
==================================================
The vast and fragmented landscape of geoscience data is severely hampered by the pervasive heterogeneity in geologic time scale terminology. This critical challenge obstructs FAIR data integration and impedes data-driven discovery, particularly for complex deep-time questions spanning diverse datasets. We introduce a novel deep-time knowledge base, meticulously constructed from knowledge graphs that correlate international and regional geologic time scales. Our core innovation lies in leveraging **temporal topology** within these graphs, enabling unprecedented sophisticated comparison and reasoning capabilities across disparate age-related geologic information throughout Earth's history. This architecture, accessible via an online service and an accompanying R package, provides a unified, queryable platform. By overcoming long-standing data integration barriers, our work significantly advances the state-of-the-art, fostering interdisciplinary research and unlocking new avenues for data-driven analyses of Earth's profound past. This foundational platform promises to revolutionize how researchers explore deep-time questions, making diverse geological data truly interoperable.
