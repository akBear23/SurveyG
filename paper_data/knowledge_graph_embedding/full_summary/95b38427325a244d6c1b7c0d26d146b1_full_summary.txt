INTRIGUING ABSTRACT:
==================================================
The relentless deluge of user-generated content (UGC) poses an unprecedented challenge to modern journalism, threatening journalistic standards with rampant misinformation, hate speech, and quality inconsistencies. Manual moderation is unsustainable, leaving media organizations struggling to effectively manage and classify this vast, continuous stream. This paper introduces a novel **participatory journalism management platform** designed to revolutionize how media handles UGC. Our innovative approach leverages advanced **semantic technologies** to enable the organized collection, intelligent classification, and efficient moderation of content in real-time. By moving beyond simple aggregation, this platform provides robust capabilities for identifying problematic content and ensuring quality control, thereby empowering journalists to maintain integrity in an increasingly participatory media landscape. This work offers a critical advancement in **automated gatekeeping** and **intelligent content management**, paving the way for future research in semantic-driven solutions for media quality assurance and combating digital disinformation.
