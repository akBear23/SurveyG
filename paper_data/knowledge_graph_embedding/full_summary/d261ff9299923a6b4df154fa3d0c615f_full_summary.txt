INTRIGUING ABSTRACT:
==================================================
Precise brain tumor segmentation from multimodal MRI is critical for clinical decision-making, yet current State-Space Models (SSMs) like Mamba struggle with the inherent inductive biases and stability required for complex visual tasks. We introduce VSMU-Net, a pioneering U-shaped network that fundamentally addresses these limitations. Our novel Inherent Inductive Bias Mamba (IB Mamba) explicitly embeds multiscale contextual and local feature information, effectively integrating convolutional inductive biases into the SSM framework. Extensive experiments on BraTS2020 and BraTS2021 datasets demonstrate VSMU-Net's superior performance over state-of-the-art methods. This work not only offers a robust and accurate solution for brain tumor segmentation but also paves the way for developing stable, visually-aware SSMs across diverse computer vision applications.
